  • expand_moreWhere are you located?
    We are located in Tamworth and we bake from our specially designed kitchen. We are registered with Tamworth Environmental Health Department as a food based business and are subject to frequent checks to ensure the safety and good hygiene of our premises.
  • expand_moreWhat are your opening times?
    We are available to be contacted:
    Mon, Tues, Thurs, Fri 9am - 5pm
    Wed 9am - 1pm
    Sat 10am - 4pm
    Sun Closed
    Bank holidays Closed
  • expand_moreHow much notice do you need?
    For wedding cakes we need at least 9 months notice. For all other cakes we need a minimum of 2 weeks notice.
  • expand_moreHow do I order?
    You can order a cake through the site using our order form. If you need to contact us about your order or prefer to talk to us in person you can call us or drop us an email using the details at the bottom of the page.
  • expand_moreDo you deliver?
    We only deliver wedding cakes and a fee may be charged if the location is far enough away. All other cakes must be picked up from our kitchen.
  • expand_moreDo you have a price list?
    If you order through our website you will get a running total as you add to and customise your order, if you order over the phone or over email we will run your order through our website and give you a running total. Wedding cakes start at £175, other cakes start at £20 and cupcakes start at £15.
  • expand_moreCan I taste cake before I buy?
    We can vouch that our cake is yummy, and we have a testimonials page where you can see that others agree, but if you wish to taste it for yourself we hold tasting sessions on the last Saturday of each month. To book a slot, drop us an email or give as a call.
  • expand_moreDo you cater to people with allergies?
    Yes we do! We can make gluten and dairy free cakes, although we will need a little extra time to ensure our kitchen has been super deep cleaned to avoid cross contamination of allergens. We regret that eggless cakes are unavailable at this time.
  • expand_moreHow long will my cake last?
    Cakes are freshly made to order with the exception of Christmas cake which is left to mature. Our cakes are never frozen and are free from preservatives, please keep your cakes in airtight containers and they will easily last for 5 days after deleivery.
  • expand_moreI would like to leave you a testimonial, how do I do that?
    Thank you very much, we are thrilled you liked our cake and service so much, please email your comments and name to us and we will add you to our page.
